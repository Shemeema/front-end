TOP 10 WRITERS IN MALAYALAM MOVIES


M.T VASUDEVAN NAIR (Writer Director Actor). He was born on 9 August 1933 in Kudallur, Kerala, British India. He is a writer and director, known for Nirmalyam (1973), Kadavu (1991) and Oru Cheru Punchiri (2000). He is married to Kalamandalam Saraswathi.

ADOOR GOPALAKRISHNAN (Writer Director Producer). He is India's most acclaimed contemporary filmmaker. Born in 1941 in Kerala, a state in south India, he belongs to a family with strong links to the performing arts, especially Kathakali, a highly-stylised form of dance drama. From the age of eight Adoor began acting for the stage, later producing and directing over twenty plays, several written by him.

K.G GEORGE (Writer Director Actor). He was born on 26 May 1946 in Changanacheri, Kerala, British India [now India]. He was a director and writer, known for Yavanika (1982), Ee Kannikoodi (1990) and Lekhayude Maranam: Oru Flashback (1983). He was married to Selma George. He died on 24 September 2023 in Kakkanad, Kerala, India.

P. PADMARAJAN (Writer Director Actor). He was an Indian author, screenwriter, and film director who was known for his landmark works in Malayalam literature and Malayalam cinema.

JOHN PAUL (Writer Producer) He was born on 29 October 1950 in India. He was a writer and producer, known for Ee Sabdam Innathe Sabdam (1985), Witness (1988) and Athirathram (1984). He was married to Aisha Elizabeth. He died on 23 April 2022 in Kochi, Kerala, India.

SREENIVASAN (Writer Director Actor). He was born on 6 April 1956 in Kannur, Kerala, India. He is an actor and writer, known for Sandesham (1991), Vadakkunokkiyantram (1989) and Akkare Akkare Akkare (1990)

A.K LOHITHADAS (Writer Director Actor). He was born on 10 May 1955 in Chalakudy, Kerala, India. He was a writer and director, known for Thaniyavartanam (1987), Kasthuriman (2003) and Joker (2000). He was married to Sindhu. He died on 28 June 2009 in Kochi, Kerala, India.

RANJITH (Writer Director Actor). He is known for Pranchiyettan and the Saint (2010), Paleri Manikyam: Oru Pathirakolapathakathinte Katha (2009) and Indian Rupee (2011).

BLESSY (Second Unit Director or Assistant Director Writer Director). He was born in Thiruvalla, Kerala, India. He is known for Kaazhcha (2004), Thanmathra (2005) and Pranayam (2011).

SHYAM PUSHKARAN (Writer Producer Additional Crew) He was born on 6 September 1984 in Cherthala, Kerala, India. He is a writer and producer, known for Kumbalangi Nights (2019), Maheshinte Prathikaaram (2016) and Joji (2021).
